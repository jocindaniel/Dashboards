**Project Overview**
This Power BI Sales Dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance across different years and product categories. The dashboard helps businesses monitor key metrics, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.

**Business Problems**
Organizations need a centralized dashboard to track sales performance, profitability, order quantity, and shipping costs. This dashboard provides an interactive view of business performance over time.

**Tools Used**
Power BI
Power Query
DAX
Excel/CSV Dataset
Data Visualization Techniques

**Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**
Total Sales: 12.64M
Total Profit: 1.47M
Total Quantity: 178K
Total Shipping Cost: 1.35M

**Dashboard Features**
Sales trend analysis over multiple years
Profit growth tracking
Category-wise sales breakdown:
Furniture
Office Supplies
Technology
Interactive KPI cards and visualizations

**Key Insights**
Sales and profits have shown consistent growth over the years.
Technology generated the highest sales among all categories.
The dashboard enables quick identification of sales trends and category performance.
